About Us

Oakland Financial Corporation is a Detroit, Michigan-based financial holding company founded in 2020. OFC is a premier Midwest bank offering consumer and commercial banking, trust and wealth management, and specialty leasing and lending products and services to consumers. OFC currently has one location only, but aims to provide most of its services online to its customers, from the ability to manage accounts, expenses, get loan estimates, as safe and secure as possible.
